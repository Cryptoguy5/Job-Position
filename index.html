Sample Job Posting

Provided below is a sample job description for an Account Manager position.

[COMPANY NAME]

Position Title: Accounting Manager

Location: City, State, Country

Duration: full-time, part-time, contract

Salary or Salary Range: Optional

Company Background

In this section, inform the candidate about your company, if possible, include the following:

  • Main Activities
  • Company goals, missions, and objectives
  • Headquarters, offices, and extent of global reach
  • Number of exployees
  • Awards, recognition of ranking (ie One of top 25 companies to work for)
  • Ethical, social, and environmental commitments
  • Past connections to interns hired in the past
  • Company quotes or tag lines
  • Corporate Website
  • Benefits of internship
